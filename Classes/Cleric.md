# Cleric
## Stats
- **HP:** 1d6+8
- **Hit Dice:** 1d8
## Class Features
- ### Channel Divinity
	- You gain the ability to conjure certain magical effects fueled by your devotion to your deity. 
	- You have 5 uses per day
